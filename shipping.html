Shipping Policy

We begin processing and shipping your order as soon as it is successfully placed and confirmed with us.

Each saree is thoughtfully handled and goes through a careful quality check before dispatch, honouring the craftsmanship and detail that goes into every piece.

Our products are neatly and securely packed to ensure they reach you in beautiful condition, just as they were intended.

We deliver across major pincodes in India. Most orders are delivered within 3–5 business days, depending on your delivery location.

To ensure a smooth and reliable delivery experience, we work with trusted and reputed courier partners across the country.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive updates to help you track your package as it makes its way to you.

Delivery timelines may vary slightly during festive seasons, high-demand periods, or due to unforeseen logistical circumstances.

A celebration of craftsmanship, delivered with care.
